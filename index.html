Common questions

Things people ask

How does Chariot track my commute automatically?

Chariot uses iOS geofencing — invisible boundaries around your home, work, and transit hubs. When your iPhone crosses one, Chariot wakes up and logs the event. No tapping required.

What is "True Cost" and why does it matter?

True Cost = cash spent + the value of your time. Most people underestimate this significantly. Chariot makes the full picture visible.

Does Chariot share my location or commute data?

Never. Chariot has no server. Your location and commute data stay on your iPhone. Nothing is shared with anyone, ever.

Why does Chariot need "Always" location permission?

iOS only delivers geofence events when your app has Always location access. Without it, Chariot can't detect when you leave or arrive.
